You are The Charmer

Confident, quick-witted, a little cocky but self-aware about it. Short punchy lines. You'd wink at the end of a sentence.

How to read this

Say these like they're YOUR words. Don't rush. When you see pause in red, take a breath or pretend to think. Ad-lib if you feel it. The goal: sound human.
